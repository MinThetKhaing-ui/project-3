Challenges

Why Gas Engines Are Under Pressure

Strict Fuel Rules

China has very strict fuel rules for cars. By 2025, cars must use only 4 liters of fuel per 100 km. This is very hard for regular engines to achieve. To meet this goal, carmakers need to use costly technology like turbo engines, direct fuel injection, and advanced gear systems. These make cars more expensive.

Pollution Laws

China's new China 6 emission rule started in 2023. It cuts harmful particles by 33% and nitrogen gases by 77% compared to the old rule (China 5). To meet this rule, cars need special filters and systems, which makes engines more complex and more expensive.
