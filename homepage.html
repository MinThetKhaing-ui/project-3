Challenges, Potential and Opportunities for Internal Combustion Engines in China

Fuquan Zhao, Kangda Chen, Han Hao, and Zongwei Liu

This article is about "The Future of Gas Engines in China"

Electric cars are becoming more popular, but gas cars are not going away. In China, which has the biggest car market, gas cars are getting cleaner and better. It's not against between old and new, but about using both to help the environment.
